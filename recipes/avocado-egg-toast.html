Avocado Toast with Egg

Home

Description

Everyone loves avocado toast, and if you add an egg to it you have a full meal. Balsamic vinegar can be used to flavor the avocado instead of the lemon juice. You can make the egg any way you like, but fried or poached gives the best results.

Ingredients

  • Butter: 1 tsp.
  • Eggs: 2 medium-sized.
  • Multigrain bread: 2 slices.
  • Avocado: 1 ripe, pitted, peeled.
  • Lemon juice: 1 tsp.
  • Cayanne pepper: 1 pinch.
  • Sea salt and black pepper: for taste.

Steps

  1. Cook the egg: Melt butter in a skillet over medium-low heat. Crack eggs into the skillet side by side and cook until eggs are white on the bottom layer and firm enough to flip, 2 to 3 minutes. Flip eggs, trying not to crack the yolk, and cook until egg reaches desired doneness, 2 to 5 minutes more.
  2. Toast the bread: Meanwhile, toast bread slices to desired doneness, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Prepare the avocado and finish: Mash avocado in a bowl; stir in lemon juice, cayenne pepper, and sea salt. Spread avocado mixture onto toast. Top with fried egg and season with sea salt and pepper.
