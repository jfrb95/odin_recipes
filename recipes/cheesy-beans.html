Cheesy Beans

Home

Description

A fantastic comfort food. This cheesy bean recipe is perfect on toast or as a dip.

Ingredients

  • Condensed cream with bacon soup: 1 can.
  • Sour cream: 1 cup.
  • Cheddar cheese: 1 cup shredded.
  • Onion: 1 tbsp minced.
  • Taco seasoning mix 1tbsp.

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients: Stir the soup, sour cream, cheese, onion, and taco seasoning together in a microwave-safe bowl.
  2. Microwave the beans: Microwave on high until hot, 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Stir the mixture: Stir and microwave again until the cheese melts and the mixture is bubbling, 3 to 5 minutes more.
